Let’s now discuss the definition of hypoglycemia.
The exact blood glucose value that defines hypoglycemia remains controversial, and there’s a lack of definitive evidence regarding at which glucose values and under what conditions neurologic damage occurs.
The S.T.A.B.L.E. Program defines hypoglycemia as “glucose delivery or availability that is inadequate to meet glucose demand.”
