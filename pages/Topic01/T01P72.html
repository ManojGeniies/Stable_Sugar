Even though we've focused on infants who are too sick to be fed, we should also discuss the IDM, LGA, SGA, and late preterm infants who are healthy enough to feed.
It's becoming common practice to treat hypoglycemia with a combination of dextrose gel and breastfeedings or formula feedings.
 
If the infant is not promptly responding to this therapy, then supportive care with IV dextrose may also be required.
