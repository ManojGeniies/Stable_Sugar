Typically, glucose screening is performed by bedside "point-of-care" testing.

  • Advantages
    • Rapid return of results
    • Minimal amount of blood is required
  • Precautions
    • Utilizes whole blood, so results may average 10% to 18% below plasma values
    • Poor accuracy of point-of-care test results when the blood sugar is low.
    • If confirmatory plasma glucose testing is done, do not delay treatment of hypoglycemia while awaiting test results.
