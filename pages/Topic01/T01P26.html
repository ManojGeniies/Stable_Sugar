Maternal indications for delivering an infant early:
  • Hypertension
  • Placental abruption
  • Placenta previa
  • Preterm rupture of membranes
  • Spontaneous labor
  • Maternal diseases that jeopardize maternal health
Fetal indications for delivering an infant early:
  • Intrauterine growth restriction
  • Complications related to multiple gestation
  • Fetal heart rate abnormalities
  • Other indications that jeopardize fetal health
