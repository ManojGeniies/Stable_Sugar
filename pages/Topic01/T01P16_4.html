Enteral feedings should also be withheld if you suspect the infant has a bowel obstruction or if there is an obvious abdominal wall defect such as gastroschisis or omphalocele.
Signs of bowel obstruction include:
  1.   Maternal history of polyhydramnios
  2.   Abdominal distension
  3.   Bilious emesis
The gastrointestinal tract may be affected from the esophagus through to the anus.
