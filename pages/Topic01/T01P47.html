Signs and symptoms that might be observed when the blood sugar is low have been divided into three main groups: general findings, neurologic signs, and cardiorespiratory signs.
General findings
  • Abnormal cry that may be weak or high-pitched
  • Poor feeding, weak suck, poor suck coordination
  • Hypothermia
  • Diaphoresis
Neurologic signs
  • Jitteriness
  • Irritability
  • Hypotonia
  • Lethargy
  • Seizures
Cardiorespiratory signs
  • Tachypnea
  • Respiratory depression
  • Apnea
  • Cyanosis
Neuroglycopenia
Inadequate glucose supply for normal brain metabolism and function.
