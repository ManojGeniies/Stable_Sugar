Once an umbilical catheter has been placed, closely monitor the catheter for inadvertent migration.
Check the centimeter marking at the umbilical cord and compare this number with prior inspections of the catheter location.
 
Maintain an airtight system and do not allow air bubbles to infuse into the baby.
 
Accidental disconnection of the catheter can cause rapid blood loss, so double-check that all tubing connections are secure.
