To provide a safe and reasonable target for treatment, The S.T.A.B.L.E. Program recommends the following:
 

For sick infants who are not well enough to be enterally fed, a glucose value of less than 50 mg/dL (2.8 mmol/L) requires corrective therapy and ongoing monitoring until the glucose is stabilized between 50 and 110 mg/dL (2.8 and 6.1 mmol/L).

Treat the blood sugar when it is < 50 mg/dL (2.8 mmol/L).
Maintain the blood sugar between 50 and 110 mg/dL (2.8 and 6.1 mmol/L).
